# 📊 Marketing & Revenue Analysis Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze marketing campaign performance and revenue generation. It helps in understanding key business metrics and identifying high-performing strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze revenue and cost efficiency
- Identify top-performing marketing channels
- Evaluate campaign performance
- Understand customer segment contribution

---

## 📁 Dataset Description
The dataset contains marketing campaign data including:

- Campaign ID
- Campaign Type
- Channel Used
- Impressions, Clicks, Conversions
- Revenue and Acquisition Cost
- ROI and Engagement Score
- Customer Segment

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Cost
- Profit
- ROI (Return on Investment)
- Conversion Rate
- Click Through Rate (CTR)

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- Revenue by Channel (Bar Chart)
- Campaign Performance Analysis
- Cost vs Revenue (Scatter Plot)
- Customer Segment Distribution
- Engagement Score Analysis
- Interactive Filters (Slicers)

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Certain channels like social media generate higher revenue
- High-cost campaigns do not always guarantee high returns
- Specific customer segments contribute significantly to revenue
- Conversion rates vary across campaign types

---

- Power BI (Data Visualization)
